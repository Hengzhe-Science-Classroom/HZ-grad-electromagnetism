# Electromagnetism (Graduate) · Interactive

**Maxwell's equations, radiation, and relativistic E&M**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 4 · Graduate

## What You'll Learn

- Electrostatics and boundary value problems
- Magnetostatics
- Maxwell's equations in matter
- Electromagnetic waves and radiation
- Waveguides and resonant cavities
- Relativistic electrodynamics

## References

- David J. Griffiths, *Introduction to Electrodynamics* (4th ed., Cambridge)
- J.D. Jackson, *Classical Electrodynamics* (3rd ed., Wiley)
- L.D. Landau & E.M. Lifshitz, *The Classical Theory of Fields* (Course of Theoretical Physics Vol. 2)
